About Srikrishna Variety Stores

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple idea to create a place where people can find all their everyday essentials in one stop. From crunchy chips and biscuits to fresh groceries, notebooks and more, we wanted to bring together the products that make daily life easier and happier.

What started as a small shop with a few shelves has now grown into a trusted store for the community. We believe in quality, affordability, and friendly service, making sure every customer feels at home when they walk through our doors.

From students preparing for exams to professionals crafting important documents, we provide the finest stationery items that make every writing experience exceptional.
